Our Staff

Jaycee Shaw

My name is Jaycee Shaw. I grew up in Nashville, Tennesee and have always had a passion for fashion. I am the head of design here at Colorado Denim. We are focused on bringing the customers the clothes they want to wear.

Shawn Bird

My name is Shawn Bird. I am from Atlanta; Georgia and I am the Marketing Manager here at Colorado Denim. I want to be able to bring attention to our and products to everyone around the world. We are passionate about our products and want our customers to be as well.

Dennis Atkins

My name is Dennis Atkins. I am a franchise owner of a Colorado Demin store in Seattle, Washington. I am focused on making each store feel unique to the city that it is in. Kristen Duran

My name is Kristen Duran. I am the Social Media Coordinator Manager at Colorado Denim. I am from Los Angles, California and have a knack for sharing my experiences with the world. I am focused our making sure our customers can see other customers wearing and enjoying our products around the world.

Brianna Mcknight

My name is Brianna McKnight. I am from Denver Colorado and I am the products design engineer. As a Colorado native I am passionate about the environment and preserving it as best as I can throughout our company.

Nathen Lang

My name is Nathen Lang. I am from Boston, Massachusetts. I am the financial coordinator at Colorado Demin. My job is to keep our products affordable for our dedicated customers. We work hard everyday to bring you the products you love and we value your continued and unwavering support.
